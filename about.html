Biography

Aqsa Khalid is a Creative Digital Media Student studying in Ireland. She has been studying in Dublin since she started her education. She went to Balagady J.N.S and S.N.S for primary education, and Adamstown C.C for secondary. She wanted to go to college as soon as possible therefore skipped her fourth year in secondary school and completed her Leaving Certificate in 2015. She is now currently pursuing her education in the Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown. Aside from her professional life, she has never stuck to a hobby for a more than a fortnight. Despising boredom, she does whatever she can do to keep herself occupied. She keeps a little notebook with her at all times to jot down new ideas or to doodle in whenever she has the time.
Key Skills: Aqsa is a perfectionist, meaning that if there is a pixel of differenece between something, she will go back and change it. She tries to keep as organised as possible by using an increduble amout of sticky notes. She can handle stress and time very well when it comes to her professional life.
